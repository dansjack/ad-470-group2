This is a chat bot that uses the SQuAD dataset and a BERT model.

You can ask this bot questions and it'll do its best to find the answer from Wikipedia

Don't know what to ask? Here are a few example questions to get started:

  • What is the biggest mammal?
  • When was the United States of America founded?
  • What is Seattle known for?
